Species

The following pages contain all the typical human species you can play, all of which give you more special abilities, some weapons, and a mysterious Revelation Seed. Choose a species for your hunter. The meaning of “human” in this world is vastly different from ours, as it has little to do with genetics. You may find out more nuances to its meaning over the course of your adventures.

Special Ability Types

All abilities are labeled with types, described as evolution, mutator, triggered, or action abilities.

  • Evolution Abilities give a permanent change or addition to one of your stats or abilities. If an ability does not list a type, it is an evolution ability.
  • Mutator Abilities give optional changes or bonuses to an action or skill check.
  • Triggered Abilities are abilities that only activate under certain conditions. Keeping a list of your hunter’s triggers can help remind you.
  • Action Abilities are used whenever you choose to. During a Boss Combat, you get two actions during your turn to use. “Free Action” abilities can be used without spending an action during Boss Combat.
